Google AdWords Tutorial

Step 1: Create a Google Account

To use Google AdWords, you need a Google account. Follow these steps:

  1. Go to Google Account Signup.
  2. Enter your first and last name.
  3. Choose a unique username.
  4. Set a strong password and confirm it.
  5. Provide a recovery email and phone number (optional but recommended).
  6. Agree to Google's terms and conditions.
  7. Click "Next" and complete the verification process.

Tips for a Smooth Signup:

  • Use a professional email address if you're creating an account for business purposes.
  • Choose a strong password with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • Verify your phone number for account security.
