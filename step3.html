Step 3: Set Up Your Campaign

Follow these steps to set up your Google Ads campaign effectively:

  1. Log into Google Ads and click on "New Campaign".
  2. Select a campaign goal (e.g., Sales, Leads, Website Traffic).
  3. Choose the campaign type: Search, Display, Video, Shopping, etc.
  4. Name your campaign for easy reference.
  5. Set up targeting options such as location, language, and audience.
  6. Define your bidding strategy and budget.
  7. Click "Next" to proceed with ad creation.

Tips for a Successful Campaign Setup:

  • Define your objectives clearly before choosing a campaign type.
  • Start with a small budget and adjust based on performance.
  • Use audience targeting to reach the right customers.
  • Experiment with different ad formats to find what works best.
