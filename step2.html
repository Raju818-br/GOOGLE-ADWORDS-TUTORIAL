Step 2: Sign in to Google Ads

After creating your Google Account, follow these steps to sign in to Google Ads:

  1. Go to Google Ads.
  2. Click on the "Sign In" button at the top right corner.
  3. Enter your Google Account email and click "Next".
  4. Enter your password and complete any security verification if required.
  5. Once logged in, you will be directed to the Google Ads dashboard.

Tips for a Smooth Sign-in:

  • Ensure your Google account is verified before signing in.
  • Use a strong password and enable two-factor authentication for better security.
  • If you forget your password, use the "Forgot password?" option to recover your account.
